A — Persistent sidebar (always open)

Sidebar stays visible on phone and tablet. Products are beside it in 2 columns. On 375px phones the sidebar would be ~110px wide — cards are narrow but the filters are always accessible.

Pros

Cons

  • Cards are cramped

C — Collapsed icon rail (expands to full sidebar)

On mobile/tablet a narrow icon rail is always visible on the left. Tap any filter group and it expands to a full sidebar overlay. On desktop it starts fully open.

  • Feels premium / editorial
