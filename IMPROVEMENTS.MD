Sirini Jewellery (sirinijewellery.com) is a Mumbai-based handcrafted fashion jewellery brand (Kundan, Meenakari, antique, gold-plated bridal pieces) with a basic but functional e-commerce structure offering core product categories, free pan-India shipping, a 7-day return policy, and simple brand storytelling, but its current digital presence appears under-optimized for discovery and conversions due to limited SEO depth, weak site architecture, and missing conversion-focused product page elements; on the website side, it can significantly improve by restructuring navigation into clear category silos (necklaces, earrings, bangles, rings), implementing clean keyword-based URLs, adding breadcrumbs and stronger internal linking between related products, and enhancing product pages with rich elements like detailed 300+ word descriptions, size guides, care instructions, high-resolution zoom images, and schema markup (Product, Review, Breadcrumb) to improve Google visibility, while also fixing technical SEO fundamentals such as Core Web Vitals (speed, mobile responsiveness, image compression, lazy loading, CDN usage, and CLS/LCP optimization) and ensuring proper indexing via XML sitemap and Google Search Console; from a pure SEO content standpoint, the site currently lacks strong keyword targeting and informational depth, so it would benefit from optimized meta titles/descriptions using high-intent terms like “Kundan bridal jewellery,” “antique gold-plated jewellery India,” and “Meenakari necklace set,” along with expansion into structured supporting pages such as category landing pages, occasion-based collections (bridal, festive), FAQs, and jewellery care/size guide pages that capture long-tail search traffic and improve topical authority; overall, the biggest opportunity lies in transforming the site from a basic product catalog into a structured SEO-driven e-commerce system where every page is intentionally built for search intent, internal linking strengthens authority flow, and product pages are enriched enough to increase both Google rankings and buyer trust, which together would directly improve organic visibility and conversion rates without needing external marketing channels.
