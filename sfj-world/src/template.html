I · About Us

The House

Saloni Fashion Jewellery is a Mumbai-based manufacturer and wholesale supplier of fashion and imitation jewellery, with a specialisation in antique jewellery. Established in 2010, the company has built a trusted reputation among retailers and distributors across India and international markets through consistent quality, diverse collections, and competitive wholesale pricing.

Every piece is designed and crafted in-house at our Santacruz (West) facility, ensuring design integrity and quality control at every stage — from raw material selection to final finishing.
