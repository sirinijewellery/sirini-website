I · Our Story

The House of Sirini

Sirini Jewellery was born in Mumbai in 2017, rooted in one simple belief — that every bride deserves to feel extraordinary. Founded by Nishit Savla, Sirini grew from years of hands-on manufacturing experience into a brand built for the women at the heart of India's most cherished celebrations.

With gold prices soaring beyond reach, we saw a gap that needed filling — not with cheaper jewellery, but with equally stunning alternatives. The same intricate designs. The same weight of tradition. A price that lets you say yes without hesitation.

III · The Craft

The Atelier

Every Sirini piece is crafted in fine brass with the same rigorous craftsmanship as gold jewellery — gleaming 22kt plating, hand-set Kundan stonework, hand-painted Meenakari enamel.

We understand the craft from the inside out — the precision, the detailing, the hours that go into every piece. Each one passes rigorous quality checks before it ships.
